You own 4 planes and have agreements to use 3 airports for passage of people and cargo.
Load your planes full and send them between airports to earn money. A plane won't make much money if it's not full. You've only got $1000, so be especially careful at the start.
How to play
Click a plane on the map or plane summary to bring up:
  • List of 'cargo' including people already on the plane
  • List of jobs waiting at the airport
  • List of airports this plane can fly to
Click the jobs waiting at the airport to load up your plane with what it can carry and click an airport to send it off! The map view will show you where the plane is.
